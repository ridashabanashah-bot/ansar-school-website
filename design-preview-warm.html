About Ansar

Built for the families of Padhinjarangadi, ready for what comes next.

Ansar School is a private, English-medium, CBSE-affiliated school for Classes I through X. We were founded by educators who believed a good school should grow with its community — not look down on it.

Our work is to prepare children — academically, ethically, and personally — for a world that's wider than where they were born, without asking them to leave home behind. We pair classical teaching with modern tools, so learning feels like part of the world children will actually live in.
