## Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our services. It also outlines your privacy rights and how the law safeguards you. By using our services, you agree to this policy.

At **Ansar School Padinjarangadi**, we are dedicated to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app or visit our website. By accessing our services or website, you agree to these terms.

## Personal Information

We may collect the personal information you voluntarily provide when you register on the app, make a purchase, or interact with us. This may include:

-   Name
-   Email address
-   Phone number
-   Payment information

## Usage Data

We may also gather information about how you use the app, including:

-   Device details (e.g., IP address, browser type, operating system)
-   Usage information (e.g., access times, pages viewed, actions taken)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for purposes such as:

-   Providing and maintaining our services
-   Improving and personalizing your experience
-   Analyzing how you use our services
-   Processing transactions and managing your orders
-   Communicating with you, including updates and promotions
-   Preventing fraud and monitoring for theft
-   Complying with legal requirements

## Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with third parties in these cases:

-   With service providers working on our behalf
-   With business partners to offer specific products or promotions
-   To meet legal obligations, respond to legal requests, or protect our rights
-   In case of a merger, sale, or transfer of assets

## Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
