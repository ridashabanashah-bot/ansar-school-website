## About Ansar School

Situated amidst the serene landscapes of Palakkad district in Kerala, Ansar School Padinjarangadi was established in 1986 by the Alfalah Charitable Trust, a non-profit organization committed to educational empowerment.

Our school is dedicated to providing quality education and support to children, particularly from economically disadvantaged backgrounds. With a holistic approach, we prioritize both academic excellence and moral development, integrating innovative teaching methodologies such as concept-based learning through multimedia resources and Cyber Square Information Technology platforms.

Our campus boasts a well-facilitated playground and a fully-equipped physical education department staffed with dedicated professionals. At Ansar School Padinjarangadi, we strive to nurture well-rounded individuals, fostering a conducive environment for learning and growth.

## Mission

Our mission is to provide a safe, nurturing, and inclusive learning environment that fosters academic excellence, personal growth, and social responsibility. We are committed to inspiring and challenging our students to become lifelong learners who are critical thinkers, effective communicators, and responsible global citizens. Through innovative and engaging teaching practices, we strive to cultivate a passion for learning and a love of knowledge in all our students.

## Vision

Our vision is to create a learning community that empowers students to become confident, compassionate, and creative individuals who are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and values to succeed in an ever-changing world.
