## School Timing

1.  School hours are from 9:25 am to 4:00 pm on ordinary working days.
2.  Every student is expected to reach the classroom before 9:25 am.
3.  Students arriving later than 9: 30 am will require permission from the Principal to enter the class by the parent’s acknowledgement in the slip available in the security cabin. Consistent late coming will result in the expulsion of the student from the school.
