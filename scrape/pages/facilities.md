### HOLISTIC DEVELOPMENT OF THE LEARNER.

Facilitating the holistic development of learners, the integrated curriculum incorporates hands-on activities to enable children to engage with their surroundings. Painting and performing arts such as lectures, quizzes, drama, singing, and dancing are seamlessly woven into the curriculum. Students' performance in these diverse activities is evaluated for grading purposes. Educational outings to cultural, historical, and ecological sites are integrated into the curriculum. Monthly festivals are celebrated with short skits performed to elucidate their significance. Participation in inter-school competitions fosters skill development. Distinguished individuals frequently visit the school to inspire and support the children's growth journey. Etiquette and codes of conduct are taught to equip children with the necessary skills to navigate various situations they may encounter. The aim is to nurture well-rounded individuals capable of effecting positive societal changes.
