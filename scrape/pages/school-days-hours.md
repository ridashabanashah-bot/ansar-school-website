## School Days and Hour

1.  The school is open six days a week, Monday to Saturday for classes III- X; Monday to Friday, five working days for Pre KG-II. School hours for students are from 9:25 AM to 4:00 PM. Students should reach the school at 9:25 AM and are expected to leave promptly at 4:00 PM.
2.  During Ramadan, the timing will differ slightly. Parents will be informed of any change in timing during Ramadan.
