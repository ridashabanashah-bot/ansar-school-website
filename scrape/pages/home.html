Welcome to

Ansar school padinjarangadi

Situated amidst the serene landscapes of Palakkad district in Kerala, Ansar School Padinjarangadi was established in 1986 by the Alfalah Charitable Trust, a non-profit organization committed to educational empowerment. Our school is dedicated to providing quality education and support to children, particularly from economically disadvantaged backgrounds. With a holistic approach, we prioritize both academic excellence and moral development, integrating innovative teaching methodologies such as concept-based learning through multimedia resources and Cyber Square Information Technology platforms. Our campus boasts a well-facilitated playground and a fully-equipped physical education department staffed with dedicated professionals. At Ansar School Padinjarangadi, we strive to nurture well-rounded individuals, fostering a conducive environment for learning and growth..

Principal’s Message

Welcome to Ansar School Padinharangadi, where education is a journey of awakening the potential within every child. In a rapidly changing world, the vision is to nurture young minds who can think deeply, act responsibly, and lead with courage and compassion. As an academic and visionary institution, the school strives to prepare learners for the demands of the 21st century. Students from Gen Z and the emerging Alpha generation are encouraged to develop critical thinking, creativity, communication, collaboration, and digital fluency so that they can thrive in an interconnected global society.​

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a learning community that empowers students to become confident, compassionate, and creative individuals who are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and values to succeed in an ever-changing world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a safe, nurturing, and inclusive learning environment that fosters academic excellence, personal growth, and social responsibility. We are committed to inspiring and challenging our students to become lifelong learners who are critical thinkers, effective communicators, and responsible global citizens. Through innovative and engaging teaching practices, we strive to cultivate a passion for learning and a love of knowledge in all our students.

Our Campus

Perched atop a hill, our campus overlooks the picturesque agricultural lands of Padinjarangadi, offering a panoramic eagle-eye view. Boasting iconic architecture, captivating landscaping, and state-of-the-art facilities including a purpose-built playground, swimming pool, and auditorium, our institution stands as the epitome of excellence in both academia and extracurricular pursuits. Join us for a journey of committed learning and unparalleled achievement.
